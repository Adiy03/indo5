What is Tapioca ?


Tapioca is a starchy substance extracted from the cassava plant's tuberous roots

It is a versatile ingredient that is widely used in culinary preparations around the world

Tapioca is popular for its unique texture and is commonly used as a thickening agent in both sweet and savory dishes
