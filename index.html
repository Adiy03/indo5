About

Indo5export is an online wholesale export trading company that offers a convenient solution for businesses to find and export products from Indonesia

With our user-friendly platform, you can easily search and select products that suit your business needs

We offer a wide selection of high quality products from various industrial sectors in Indonesia especially agriculture

Ensures that every product exported through us meets high quality and safety standards

Services

We provide services for our customers to easily find products, especially natural products, in Indonesia for export needs

We also provide services to help with the certification and delivery of the products you want to export

Shipping

For some product we accept LCL (Less Than Container Load) shipping terms to make it easier for overseas micro small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) can also get our products

Payment

We use a payment method with a minimum
advance payment of 20% of the agreed price for the first and balance payment before shipment

Most of our transactions are conducted using
Wise transfers as the preferred payment method.

Delivery

There are several shipping points because Indonesia has 5 big islands, but we often ship through the ports of Tanjung Priok Jakarta for products located in the western and Tanjung Perak Surabaya for the eastern part of Indonesia
