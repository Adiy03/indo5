Shipping

For some product we accept LCL (Less Than Container Load) shipping terms to make it easier for overseas micro small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) can also get our products

Payment

We use a payment method with a minimum
advance payment of 10% of the agreed price for the first and balance payment before shipment

A 10% advance payment that has been given is used as proof of seriousness in the transaction, used for operational activities and cannot be reversed by cancellation of orders by importers

Delivery
