Skipjack tuna is one of the most commercially important tuna species. It is commonly caught for canned tuna production

The majority of skipjack tuna caught is processed and canned for human consumption. Canned skipjack tuna is a staple in many households worldwide and is used in a variety of dishes, including salads, sandwiches, casseroles, and pasta

Skipjack tuna is also used in the preparation of sashimi and sushi. While other tuna species like yellowfin and bluefin are often preferred for high-end sushi, skipjack tuna is still used in various sushi dishes

Skipjack tuna is processed into various frozen and fresh products for both domestic and international markets. These products include fillets, loins, and steaks that cater to different consumer preferences
