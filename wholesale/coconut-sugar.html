Indonesia Wholesale Trade: Coconut Sugar for Sale


Coconut sap sugar. HS Code: 17029051

Coconut sugar, also known as coconut palm sugar, is a natural sweetener derived from the sap of the flower bud stem of the coconut palm tree

Coconut sugar has gained popularity as an alternative to refined white sugar due to its perceived health benefits

Coconut Sugar Uses:


Sweetener in Food Products: Coconut sugar is used as a sweetener in various food products, including baked goods, confectionery, and desserts. It adds a rich, caramel-like flavor to dishes

Beverages: Coconut sugar can be used to sweeten beverages such as tea, coffee, and smoothies. Its distinct flavor profile enhances the taste of these drinks

Natural and Healthier Sweetener: As a natural sweetener with a lower glycemic index compared to refined sugars, coconut sugar is often incorporated into health-conscious and organic products, such as energy bars, protein snacks, and dietary supplements

Organic and Specialty Foods: Coconut sugar is often featured in the formulation of organic and specialty food products, catering to consumers seeking natural, minimally processed sweeteners

Barbecue Sauces and Marinades: Coconut sugar is used in the preparation of barbecue sauces and marinades, contributing a sweet and savory taste to these culinary products

Healthy Snacks: Coconut sugar is incorporated into various healthy snacks, such as granola bars, trail mixes, and dried fruit snacks, offering a natural sweetness to these products

Indonesia Coconut Sugar for Sale:


Indonesia is one of the largest producers of coconuts globally. The country's vast coconut plantations provide a sustainable and abundant source of coconut sap, the raw material used to produce coconut sugar

Indonesia is home to various coconut varieties, each with its unique flavor profile. This diversity allows for the production of coconut sugar with different taste characteristics, catering to a broad range of consumer preferences

Many coconut sugar producers in Indonesia adhere to organic and sustainable farming practices. This can be a significant advantage in markets where consumers prioritize products that are environmentally friendly and produced without the use of synthetic chemicals
