Pomelos. HS Code: 080540

Pomelos are a type of citrus fruit. They are the largest citrus fruits, resembling a big grapefruit, and are native to Southeast Asia such as Indonesia

Pomelos are known for their thick rind and a sweet, mild, and slightly acidic flavor. They are typically green or yellow in color, with a sweet and mild taste that is less bitter than a grapefruit

Pomelos are often enjoyed fresh, either on their own or in fruit salads, and are also used in various culinary dishes and beverages for their unique flavor and fragrance

Pomelos are used in preparing various dishes such as salads, desserts, and marmalades. Their sweet and tangy flavor adds a unique touch to many recipes

Pomelos are used to produce refreshing and nutritious juice, which is often consumed fresh or used in the beverage industry to create mixed fruit juices, and other drinks

The essential oil extracted from pomelos is used in the flavoring and fragrance industry. It is utilized in the production of perfumes, cosmetics, and various food products for its pleasant and distinct aroma

Pomelos are a rich source of vitamin C and other nutrients. They are used in the production of dietary supplements and health products, contributing to the overall wellness and immune support of consumers
