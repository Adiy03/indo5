Crude Coconut Oil. HS Code: 151311

Crude coconut oil is the unrefined, unprocessed oil extracted from the meat of mature coconuts

Crude coconut oil typically has a distinct coconut aroma and flavor, and it solidifies at cooler temperatures due to its high saturated fat content

It is rich in medium-chain fatty acids, particularly lauric acid, which is known for its potential health benefits

Although crude coconut oil is not suitable for direct consumption, it serves as a fundamental ingredient in the production of various coconut-based products and derivatives

It is widely used in the food industry for cooking, baking, and as an ingredient in the manufacturing of processed foods. Additionally, it is utilized in the production of cosmetics, soaps, and personal care products due to its moisturizing and emollient properties

Moreover, crude coconut oil can be further processed through refining methods to produce refined, bleached, and deodorized (RBD) coconut oil

Indonesia is one of the leading producers of coconuts globally, with a significant supply of high-quality coconuts. This abundant availability of coconuts ensures a steady and reliable source of raw material for the production of crude coconut oil

Indonesian crude coconut oil is known for its high quality and diverse applications. It is widely used in the food industry, especially in the production of various food products and as an ingredient in cooking and baking
