Coconut oil and its fractions, whether or not refined (excl. crude). HS Code: 151319

RBD coconut oil refers to the type of coconut oil that has undergone the refining, bleaching, and deodorizing (RBD) process

RBD coconut oil is derived from the kernel or meat of mature coconuts and is processed to remove impurities and achieve a more neutral flavor and aroma

The refining process involves several steps to produce a final product that is suitable for various industrial and culinary applications

The RBD coconut oil is widely used in the food industry for cooking, frying, baking, and as an ingredient in various processed foods. It has a higher smoke point and a more neutral taste compared to unrefined coconut oil, making it suitable for applications that require a stable and flavor-neutral cooking oil

Additionally, RBD coconut oil is used in the production of cosmetics, soaps, and personal care products, where a neutral scent and appearance are desirable

Indonesia is one of the largest producers of coconuts globally, providing a consistent and abundant supply of raw materials for the production of RBD coconut oil

The country's favorable tropical climate and suitable geographical conditions contribute to the abundant availability of coconuts, ensuring a steady and reliable source for global market
