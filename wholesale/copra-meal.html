Copra Meal Uses

Animal Feed:
Copra meal is an essential ingredient in animal feed formulations for livestock, poultry, and aquaculture. It provides a good source of protein, fiber, and fats, making it a valuable and cost-effective supplement in animal diets

Organic Fertilizer:
Copra meal is utilized in organic farming as a slow-release fertilizer. It helps enrich the soil with nutrients and organic matter, improving soil fertility and enhancing plant growth

Industrial Applications:
Copra meal can be utilized in various industrial processes, such as the production of activated carbon and other industrial applications that require fibrous materials
