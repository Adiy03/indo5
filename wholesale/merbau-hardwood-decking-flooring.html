Merbau Timber Wood for Sale


Jongkong (Dactylocladus spp.) and Merbau (Intsia spp.), planed, sanded or end-jointed. HS Code: 44072991

Merbau timber refers to wood sourced from the Merbau tree (Intsia spp.), a tropical hardwood tree native to the Pacific islands, Southeast Asia

It is known for its exceptional strength, durability, and natural resistance to termites, making it a popular choice for various construction and woodworking applications

Merbau Timber Wood Uses:


Outdoor Decking: Merbau is widely used for outdoor decking due to its resistance to weathering, decay, and insects. Its natural oils make it highly durable, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial decking projects

Flooring: Merbau timber is a popular choice for flooring, especially in high-traffic areas. Its hardness and resistance to wear and tear make it suitable for flooring applications in homes, offices, and public spaces

Furniture: The durability and attractive grain pattern of Merbau make it an excellent choice for crafting furniture. It is commonly used in the production of cabinets, tables, chairs, and other indoor and outdoor furniture

Doors and Windows: Merbau timber is often used in the construction of doors and windows. Its stability and resistance to warping make it a reliable choice for creating sturdy and long-lasting door and window frames

Cladding: Merbau can be used as cladding for both interior and exterior walls. Its natural resistance to decay and insects makes it suitable for protecting the structure while providing an aesthetically pleasing appearance

Boat Building: Due to its natural resistance to water and decay, Merbau is sometimes used in boat building. Its durability and strength make it suitable for various marine applications

Fencing: Merbau is used for fencing due to its durability and resistance to termites. It can withstand exposure to the elements, making it a reliable choice for creating long-lasting fences

Pergolas and Gazebos: Merbau timber is often chosen for constructing pergolas and gazebos in outdoor spaces. Its strength and resistance to weather conditions make it suitable for supporting structures in gardens and outdoor landscapes

Stairs and Balustrades: The hardness of Merbau makes it suitable for use in stairs and balustrades. Its strength ensures that these structural elements can withstand regular use and remain aesthetically pleasing over time

Musical Instruments: In some cases, Merbau is used in the construction of musical instruments, such as guitars and drums, due to its acoustic properties and durability
