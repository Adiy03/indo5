Areca Nut for Sale


Nuts n.e.s. HS Code: 0802Xd

Areca nuts, also known as betel nuts, are the seeds of the Areca catechu palm tree

Areca nuts are small, hard, and typically round or oval in shape. They have a reddish-brown outer covering that needs to be removed before consumption

Areca Nut Uses:


Food and Flavor Industry: Areca nuts are sometimes used in the food industry as flavoring agents. They may be included in traditional dishes and sweets for their unique taste

Chewing Tobacco and Betel Quid Industry: The most well-known use of areca nuts is in the production of betel quid, a mixture of areca nut, betel leaf, slaked lime, and often tobacco

Tanning Industry: Areca nut extracts contain tannins, which are substances used in the tanning industry. Tannins are known for their ability to bind to proteins, making them useful in the process of turning animal hides into leather
