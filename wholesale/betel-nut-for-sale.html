Areca nuts, also known as betel nuts, are the seeds of the Areca catechu palm tree

Areca nuts are small, hard, and typically round or oval in shape. They have a reddish-brown outer covering that needs to be removed before consumption

Areca nuts has stimulant properties and can induce feelings of alertness and mild euphoria. This stimulant effect is one reason for their widespread use in certain cultural and social contexts

Apart from their use as a stimulant in cultural practices, areca nuts are not commonly used as a culinary ingredient in the same way as other nuts. However, they might occasionally be used in some regional cuisines for their unique flavor and texture
