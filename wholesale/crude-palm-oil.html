Crude palm oil. HS Code: 151110

Crude palm oil (CPO) is a type of vegetable oil that is derived from the fruit of the oil palm tree

It is obtained through the extraction and pressing of the palm fruit, resulting in a crude, unrefined oil that has a reddish-orange color and a semi-solid consistency at room temperature due to its high saturated fat content

CPO serves as a crucial ingredient in the food industry and is utilized in the production of a wide range of food products, such as cooking oil, margarine, shortening, and various processed foods. Its semi-solid texture at room temperature makes it suitable for applications that require a stable fat base

CPO is also utilized in the manufacturing of cosmetics and personal care products, including soaps, lotions, and other skincare items. Its moisturizing properties and emollient characteristics make it a valuable ingredient in various beauty and personal care formulations

In addition to its use in the food and cosmetics industries, CPO is increasingly utilized in the production of biofuels, particularly biodiesel. Its potential as a renewable energy source has led to increased interest in utilizing palm oil as a sustainable alternative to conventional fossil fuels

Crude palm oil finds application in various industrial sectors for the production of lubricants, candles, and other industrial products that require a stable and versatile base material

In Indonesia benefits from an abundant supply of palm oil, owing to its extensive cultivation of oil palm trees across the country. This consistent and substantial supply allows Indonesia to meet the global demand for palm oil, making it a reliable and stable exporter in the international market
