Longans for Sale


Longans. HS Code: 08134010

Longans are tropical fruits that belong to the soapberry family and are closely related to lychees and rambutans

They are native to Southern Asia and Longans are known for their sweet and musky flavor

They have a translucent white flesh surrounding a single seed. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a thin, brittle, and brownish skin that is easily removed

Longans Uses:


Fresh Snacking: The simplest way to enjoy longans is by peeling and eating them fresh. They have a sweet and floral flavor, making them a delightful snack

Fruit Salads: Longans can be added to fruit salads to enhance their sweetness and provide a unique texture. Combine them with other tropical fruits for a refreshing salad

Desserts and Sweets: Longans can be used in various desserts, including puddings, ice creams, sorbets, and gelatin-based treats. Their natural sweetness adds a pleasant taste to these dishes

Canned or Dried Longans: Longans can be canned or dried for preservation. Canned longans are convenient for use in desserts and beverages, while dried longans can be enjoyed as a chewy snack or rehydrated for cooking

Beverages: They can be used in cocktails, smoothies, fruit punches, or simply infused into water for a refreshing drink

Jams and Preserves: Longans can be used to make jams, jellies, or preserves. Their natural sweetness makes for a delightful spread on toast or as an accompaniment to cheese

Culinary Garnish: Longans can be used as a garnish for both sweet and savory dishes. They add visual appeal and a burst of sweetness to salads, appetizers, or main courses

Longan Tea: Longans can be used to make a fragrant and sweet tea. Simply steep dried longans in hot water for a soothing beverage
