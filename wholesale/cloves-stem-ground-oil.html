Cloves (whole fruit, cloves and stems). HS Code: 0907

Cloves are the aromatic flower buds of the Syzygium aromaticum tree, which is native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia. They are known for their strong, warm, and sweet flavor, as well as their rich aroma

Cloves are harvested before they bloom and are dried to obtain their distinctive brown color. They are a versatile spice used in both whole and ground forms and are popular in various cuisines around the world

In culinary applications, cloves are commonly used in both sweet and savory dishes, including baked goods, sauces, curries, and marinades, adding depth and complexity to the flavor profile. They are often included in spice blends and used to infuse flavor into beverages like tea

Cloves are also utilized in the production of essential oils, perfumes, and various natural remedies for their aromatic and therapeutic qualities

Indonesia's robust cloves industry plays a significant role in the global spice market, providing high-quality cloves for culinary, medicinal, and commercial uses while emphasizing sustainable agricultural practices and the responsible management of natural resources
