Cloves (whole fruit, cloves and stems). HS Code: 0907

Cloves are the aromatic flower buds which is native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia. They are known for their strong, warm, and sweet flavor, as well as their rich aroma

Cloves are a versatile spice used in both whole and ground forms and are popular in various cuisines around the world

Cloves Uses:


Spice in Cooking: Cloves are a popular spice in both sweet and savory dishes. They are often used in the preparation of stews, soups, curries, rice dishes, and pickles, adding a warm and aromatic flavor

Baking: Cloves are a common ingredient in baking, especially in recipes for cakes, cookies, and gingerbread. They contribute a distinct and spicy flavor

Mulled Drinks: Cloves are a key component of cider, and other hot beverages. They add depth and warmth to the flavor profile

Spice Blends: Cloves are a component of garam masala, a spice blend used in many Indian dishes. and Cloves are often included in pumpkin pie spice blends used in baking and seasonal recipes

Preservation and Pickling: Cloves are used in pickling spice blends for preserving fruits, vegetables, and meats

Clove Oil: Clove essential oil is extracted from the buds and is used in aromatherapy, massage, and as a flavoring agent in some products

Cigarette Manufacturing: Cloves are sometimes used in the production of clove cigarettes, also known as kretek. The cloves contribute to the distinctive flavor of these cigarettes

Beverages: Cloves are a common ingredient in chai tea, adding a warm and spicy flavor. Cloves are often used in the preparation ciders, and other hot beverages during colder seasons
