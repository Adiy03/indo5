Crumb Rubber for Sale


Waste & scrap of soft rubber.
HS Code: 400400

Crumb rubber granules and powder are products derived from the recycling process of rubber tires or other rubber products. These materials are produced by shredding or grinding rubber into small, uniform particles of various sizes

Crumb Rubber Granules and Powder Uses:


Rubberized Mulch: Ground-up rubber from tires is used as rubberized mulch in landscaping and playgrounds. This mulch provides an alternative to traditional wood mulch, offering better durability and reduced maintenance

Tire Manufacturing: Crumb rubber can be used as a raw material in the manufacturing of new tires. It is often mixed with virgin rubber to create a rubber compound that meets specific performance and durability standards

Asphalt Rubber: Crumb rubber is used in the production of asphalt rubber, a type of road pavement. The addition of crumb rubber to asphalt enhances the properties of the pavement, providing improved elasticity, durability, and resistance to cracking

Athletic Surfaces: Crumb rubber is commonly used in the construction of athletic surfaces, such as running tracks, playgrounds, and sports fields. The elastic properties of crumb rubber provide cushioning and shock absorption, reducing the risk of injuries

Flooring Products: Crumb rubber is used in the manufacturing of various flooring products, including rubber tiles and mats. These products are popular in gyms, fitness centers, and other high-impact areas due to their shock-absorbing properties

Construction Materials: Crumb rubber can be incorporated into construction materials such as concrete to enhance properties like flexibility and crack resistance. It is also used in soundproofing materials

Automotive Parts: Some automotive parts, such as mud flaps and splash guards, are made using crumb rubber. These components benefit from the material's flexibility and resistance to weathering

Recycled Rubber Products: Various recycled rubber products, including doormats, garden edging, and rubberized roofing materials, are made from crumb rubber
