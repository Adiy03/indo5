Crumb rubber granules and powder are products derived from the recycling process of rubber tires or other rubber products

These materials are produced by shredding or grinding rubber into small, uniform particles of various sizes

Crumb rubber granules come in various sizes, typically ranging from small granules to larger particles, depending on the specific application. Crumb rubber powder, on the other hand, has a finer texture, resembling a powder or fine granular form

Crumb rubber granules and powder are used in a wide range of applications, including sports surfaces such as artificial turf and running tracks, as well as in the manufacturing of rubber products, construction materials, and various industrial and commercial applications. They are also utilized in the production of rubber-modified asphalt for road construction, and etc

These materials retain the inherent properties of rubber, including its elasticity, resilience, and durability. They provide enhanced shock absorption and impact resistance, making them suitable for various impact-absorbing and protective applications

Indonesia is one of the significant producers of crumb rubber, contributing to the global market supply. Crumb rubber production in Indonesia is a part of the country's efforts to recycle rubber materials and contribute to sustainable practices in the rubber industry
