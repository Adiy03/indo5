Running Track Surfaces: Crumb rubber granules are incorporated into running track surfaces to provide a comfortable, shock-absorbing track for athletes

Indoor Sports Flooring: Rubberized sports flooring in gyms and indoor sports facilities often includes crumb rubber to reduce impact and provide resilience

Asphalt Modification: Crumb rubber powder is added to asphalt mixtures to improve road durability and reduce road noise. This process is known as rubberized asphalt

Industrial Flooring: Rubberized coatings are applied to industrial floors to create non-slip and impact-resistant surfaces

Automotive Parts: Crumb rubber can be used in the manufacturing of various automotive parts, including mud flaps, bumpers, and seals

Noise Barriers: Crumb rubber can be used as a component in noise barriers along highways to reduce traffic noise

Vibration Isolation: Rubber granules can be used to isolate vibrations in buildings and machinery

Mulch: Rubber mulch made from crumb rubber is used in landscaping as an alternative to wood mulch. It is long-lasting and does not decompose
