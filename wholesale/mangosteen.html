Mangosteen is often referred to as the "queen of fruits" due to its exquisite taste and the various health benefits it offers

Mangosteen is typically consumed fresh as a dessert fruit. It can also be used to make juices, smoothies, and jams

Extracts from mangosteen fruit and seeds are used in the cosmetic industry for their antioxidant and skin-nourishing properties, appearing in skincare products like lotions and creams
