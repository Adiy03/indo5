Ginger is a flowering plant that belongs to the Zingiberaceae family. The plant is native to Southeast Asia

Ginger has a distinctive flavor and aroma that is both spicy and slightly sweet. It has been used for centuries in various cuisines and traditional medicine

Ginger Uses:


Beverage Production: Ginger is used in the production of various beverages, including ginger ale, ginger beer, and ginger-flavored liqueurs. It adds a distinctive flavor and warmth to these drinks

Baked Goods: Ginger is a common ingredient in baked goods such as gingerbread cookies, cakes, and muffins. Its flavor adds a unique twist to these treats

Flavoring and Fragrance: Ginger is used in the flavoring and fragrance industry to add a spicy and aromatic note to products. It can be found in perfumes, cosmetics, soaps, and other scented products

Confectionery Industry: Ginger is used to make various types of candies, such as ginger chews, crystallized ginger, and ginger-flavored chocolates. These products are often enjoyed for their sweet and spicy taste

Health Supplements: Ginger extract and ginger powder are used as dietary supplements. They are available in capsule or powder form and are marketed for their potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and digestive properties

Food Processing: Ginger is used as an ingredient in processed foods such as sauces, marinades, and dressings to enhance flavor profiles and add a hint of spiciness

Indonesia Ginger


Elephant Ginger:


This is one of the most common and popular varieties of ginger in Indonesia. It is known for its large size and distinctive flavor. Jahe Gajah is often used in culinary dishes, herbal remedies, and for making ginger-based products

Emprit Ginger:


This variety of ginger is smaller in size compared to Jahe Gajah. It has a spicier and more intense flavor, making it a preferred choice for adding a strong ginger flavor to dishes and products

Aromatic Ginger:


Also known as "perfumed ginger," this variety is valued for its pleasant fragrance and milder flavor. It is often used in making herbal teas, traditional drinks, and cosmetics

Red Ginger:


Red ginger is characterized by its reddish skin and pinkish hues on the inside. It is used both for culinary purposes and for its potential health benefits. The distinct color makes it visually appealing in various applications
