Frozen Shrimp Uses :


Shrimp Scampi: Shrimp scampi is a classic Italian-American dish where shrimp are sautéed in a garlic and butter or olive oil sauce, often with white wine and lemon juice. It's typically served over pasta or with crusty bread

Shrimp Cocktail: Shrimp cocktail is a popular appetizer where cooked and chilled shrimp are served with a tangy cocktail sauce made from ketchup, horseradish, and other seasonings

Shrimp Stir-Fry: Shrimp are commonly used in stir-fry dishes, where they are quickly cooked with vegetables and various sauces like soy sauce and ginger. This is often served over rice or noodles

Shrimp Scampi Pizza: Shrimp can also be used as a pizza topping. Shrimp scampi pizza typically includes shrimp, garlic, butter, and sometimes a white sauce, all on a pizza crust

Shrimp Tacos: Grilled or sautéed shrimp can be used as a filling for tacos. They are often paired with fresh salsa, avocado, lettuce, and a variety of toppings

Shrimp Curry: Shrimp can be added to a variety of curry dishes, such as Thai green curry or Indian shrimp curry. The shrimp absorb the flavors of the spices and sauce

Shrimp Po' Boy: A shrimp po' boy is a sandwich originating from Louisiana, USA. It consists of deep-fried shrimp served in a baguette or French bread with lettuce, tomato, and a creamy remoulade sauce

Shrimp and Grits: This is a classic Southern dish where shrimp are served over creamy, cheesy grits, often with a flavorful sauce or gravy

Shrimp Fried Rice: Shrimp can be a key protein component in fried rice dishes, along with vegetables, rice, and seasonings

Shrimp Pasta: Shrimp is frequently used in pasta dishes, such as shrimp Alfredo or shrimp scampi pasta. These dishes often feature a creamy or garlic-based sauce

Shrimp Salad: Shrimp can be added to salads for added protein and flavor. They pair well with a variety of greens, vegetables, and salad dressings

Shrimp and Vegetable Skewers: Grilled or broiled shrimp can be skewered with vegetables and marinated before cooking. This is a healthy and flavorful option
