Avocados. HS Code: 080440

Avocado is a unique fruit known for its creamy texture, rich flavor, and high nutrient content

Avocados are typically pear-shaped and have a green or blackish skin, which may vary in texture depending on the variety. The flesh of the avocado is smooth and buttery

Due to their mild taste and creamy consistency, avocados are a versatile ingredient used in a wide range of culinary applications. They are frequently incorporated into salads, sandwiches, wraps, and dips like guacamole

Avocado toast, a popular breakfast or snack option, involves spreading mashed or sliced avocado onto toasted bread and often garnishing it with various seasonings, such as salt, pepper, or red pepper flakes

Avocados are often used in smoothies and juices to add a creamy texture and nutritional value. They contribute healthy fats and a smooth consistency to beverages

Avocado oil, derived from the fruit, is used in the skincare industry for its moisturizing properties. It is often found in creams, lotions, and other beauty products

Avocado oil is also used in hair care products for its nourishing and conditioning effects on hair

The versatility and nutritional benefits of avocados have led to their widespread use across diverse industries, making them a valuable commodity with a range of applications beyond traditional culinary uses

With its tropical climate and fertile soil, Indonesia provides an ideal environment for avocado cultivation, resulting in high-quality produce known for its distinct taste and nutritional value
