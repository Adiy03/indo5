Moringa Uses:


Tea: Moringa leaves can be used to make moringa tea. This involves steeping dried moringa leaves in hot water, similar to brewing traditional herbal tea. Moringa tea is known for its potential health benefits and is consumed for its nutritional content

Oil Production: Moringa seeds can be cold-pressed to extract moringa oil. This oil is used in cooking, salad dressings, and as a base for skincare products due to its high levels of monounsaturated fats and antioxidants

Dietary Supplement: Moringa leaves are often dried and ground into a fine powder to create moringa leaf powder. This powder can be used as a dietary supplement, either by adding it to smoothies, juices, or sprinkling it on food. It's a convenient way to increase your intake of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants

Nutrient-Rich Food: Moringa leaves and pods can be cooked and incorporated into various dishes, such as soups, stews, curries, and stir-fries. They add a nutritious boost to meals and are particularly valuable in regions with limited access to fresh vegetables

Cosmetics: Moringa oil is used in the cosmetics industry to make skincare products such as lotions, moisturizers, and hair care products due to its moisturizing and antioxidant properties

Water Purification: Moringa seeds contain natural coagulants that can help purify water. Crushed moringa seeds can be added to turbid water to bind with impurities and sediment, making it easier to filter and drink clean water

Moringa products:


Moringa Leaves (Dried): HS Code 1211.90
This code is for dried leaves of plants, including Moringa leaves

Moringa Leaf Powder: HS Code 1211.90.20
This code is for dried, crushed or ground Moringa leaves, specifically in powder form

Moringa Oil: HS Code 1515.90.90
This code is for vegetable fats and oils, including Moringa oil

Moringa Seeds: HS Code 1207.91
This code is for seeds of plants used primarily for sowing purposes but can also include Moringa seeds for other purposes

Moringa Capsules (Dietary Supplements): HS Code 2106.90
This code is for food preparations, including dietary supplements, which can include Moringa capsules
