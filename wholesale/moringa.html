Moringa is a versatile and nutrient-rich plant that often referred to as the "drumstick tree". While the leaves of the moringa tree are the most commonly consumed part, other parts, such as the seeds, flowers, and roots, are also edible and have culinary uses in various cultures

Moringa leaves are highly nutritious and are a good source of essential vitamins and minerals, including vitamin C, vitamin A, potassium, calcium, and iron. They are also rich in antioxidants and contain all nine essential amino acids, making it a complete protein source

Moringa is believed to offer various health benefits, such as improving energy levels, promoting healthy skin, supporting digestion, and boosting the immune system. Its antioxidant properties may help protect cells from oxidative damage

Moringa supplements, such as moringa powder or capsules, are marketed for their nutritional value and potential health benefits

Moringa powder is a fine, green powder made from the dried leaves of the moringa tree (Moringa oleifera). It is a popular dietary supplement and superfood known for its exceptional nutritional content and potential health benefits

Moringa oleifera capsules are dietary supplements made from the dried and powdered leaves of the moringa tree (Moringa oleifera). These capsules are a convenient way to incorporate the nutritional benefits of moringa into your diet, especially if you don't have access to fresh moringa leaves or prefer a more concentrated form of consumption
